Heart disease is considered as one of the major causes of death throughout the world. As doctors deal with precious human life, it is very important for them to be right their results.

Thus we at Pulse, have developed a machine learning algorithm which can predict the vulnerability of heart disease, given basic symptoms like age, gender, pulse rate, resting blood pressure, cholesterol, fasting blood sugar, resting electrocardiographic results, exercise induced angina, ST depression ST segment the slope at peak exercise, number of major vessels coloured by fluoroscopy and maximum heart rate achieved.
